## CONVEYOR TYPES (one per type page)

### P1 — Launch

- [ ] **Roller conveyor** — Cutaway showing gravity vs powered vs MDR internals. 3 variants side-by-side. Labeled callouts: frame, rollers, bearings, drive chain.
- [ ] **Gravity roller** — Side-view: slight decline, boxes riding, arrows showing gravity flow. Labels: decline angle, roller pitch, product weight zone.
- [ ] **Powered / live roller** — MDR cutaway: internal brushless motor, zone control wiring. Top-down 4-zone accumulation view.
- [ ] **Accumulation conveyor** — Top-down ZPA with 5 zones. Products queued with gaps. Sensors visible. Before/after: without vs with accumulation.
- [ ] **Transfer conveyor** — 4-panel: pop-up wheels, right-angle chain, turntable, push diverter. Top-down with directional arrows.
- [ ] **CDLR conveyor** — Side cutaway: chain loop under rollers, sprocket drive, heavy pallet on top. Callouts: drive sprocket, chain, roller, bearing, frame.
- [ ] **MDR conveyor** — Cutaway of single MDR roller (internal motor, control card, 24V wiring). Second diagram: top-down 4-zone MDR with products in zones.
- [ ] **Live roller conveyor** — Belt-driven live roller cross-section. Show belt loop driving rollers from underneath.
- [ ] **Vertical reciprocating conveyor (VRC)** — Side view: platform between two masts, safety gates at top/bottom levels.

- [ ] **Drag chain conveyor** — Side cutaway: enclosed tubular housing, chain pulling material through tube.

- [ ] **Flexible/expandable** — Two-state: collapsed (4ft) and expanded (20ft) reaching into truck at loading dock.
- [ ] **Screw conveyor** — Longitudinal cutaway: trough with helical auger, drive motor. Second panel: 4 flight types (standard, cut, ribbon, paddle).
- [ ] **Bucket elevator** — Side cutaway: boot section, vertical casing with buckets on belt, head discharge.
- [ ] **Flat belt / slider bed** — Simple side cutaway: belt on slider bed, drive pulley, idler, product on top.
- [ ] **Cleated conveyor** — Side view: belt on 30° incline, cleats holding products. Inset: 3 cleat profiles (T, V, bucket).
- [ ] **Wire mesh conveyor** — Side view: wire mesh belt with airflow arrows passing through, food products on top in cooling tunnel.
- [ ] **Hinged steel belt** — Close-up: interlocking steel plates with hinge pins. Second view: under CNC catching chips.

- [ ] **Vacuum conveyor** — Vertical vacuum tube with powder being lifted from drum to hopper above.
- [ ] **Slat conveyor** — Heavy-duty slat links on chain, hot/heavy product riding on flat surface.

## CONVEYOR FUNCTIONS

- [ ] **Tray loading** — Tray denester → conveyor → pick-and-place loading products → loaded tray exits.
- [ ] **Stacking machine** — Products arriving single-file, being counted and stacked into groups.
- [ ] **Infeed/outfeed** — Timing screw creating exact product spacing to match machine cycle rate.
- [ ] **Star wheel feeder** — Star wheel/disc feeder timing bottles into filler.
- [ ] **Product handling** — 4-panel: turning, flipping, inverting, orienting a box on conveyor.
- [ ] **Spreader conveyor** — Products going from single-file to spread across full belt width.
- [ ] **Indexing conveyor** — Products at exact equal spacing, robot arm descending at one position. Motion profile inset.
- [ ] **Storage/buffer** — Isometric spiral accumulator: products entering bottom, spiraling up, exiting top. FIFO arrows.
- [ ] **Ball transfer table** — Top-down: grid of ball units, box with multi-directional arrows. Inset: ball unit cross-section.
- [ ] **Reject system** — Pneumatic pusher rejecting failed product off main conveyor into reject bin.
- [ ] **Collating/laning** — Single infeed splitting into 4 lanes via chicane/serpentine routing.
- [ ] **Slug loading** — Products grouped into slug, slug pushed into case.
- [ ] **Pusher/diverter** — Pneumatic pusher bar diverting product from main line to branch.
- [ ] **Singulation** — Multi-lane to single-lane funneling for scanning/labeling.
- [ ] **Metering** — Conveyor with speed differential creating exact gaps between products.
- [ ] **Bag loading** — Pouches/bags being loaded into case by automated system.
- [ ] **Inspection/metal detection** — Conveyor passing through metal detector arch, checkweigher section.
