## Site Structure Overview

```
packagingconveyor.com/
├── / (homepage)
├── /conveyor-types/ (category)
│   ├── 20 hub pages (individual conveyor types)
│   └── 5 sub-pages (variants/subtypes)
├── /industries/ (category)
│   ├── 3 hub pages (food, packaging lines, pharma)
│   └── 7 sub-pages (sanitary, bakery, cooling, beverage, pallet, case)
├── /conveyor-functions/ (category)
│   └── 7 hub pages (tray loading, stacking, infeed, handling, indexing, storage, ball transfer)
└── /resources/ (category)
    ├── 3 hub pages (automation, directory, design guide)
    └── 2 sub-pages (custom, components)
```
